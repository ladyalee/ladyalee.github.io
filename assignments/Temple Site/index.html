The Kansas City Temple is located in Missouri and is the 137th operating temple for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. It was dedicated on May 6, 2012 by President Thomas S. Monson. Sitting just west of Liberty Jail with its two majestic double towers. Many trees and flowers cover the ground for both attendees and the public to enjoy.
